Our Philosophy

Fragrance as Memory

A perfume is not merely a scent — it is a fragment of time, preserved in amber. At Aurum Noir, we believe in the ancient alchemy of perfumery: the slow marriage of rare ingredients, the patience of maceration, the precision of the nose.

Our master perfumers spend years in Grasse, Oman, and Tokyo — sourcing what cannot be synthesized, learning what cannot be taught.

Rare natural ingredients, ethically sourced
No synthetic base notes — ever
Each batch crafted in small quantities
Aged minimum 6 months before bottling
